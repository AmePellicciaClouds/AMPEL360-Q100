# ATA 53-00 — Fuselage (General)

**ATA Chapter 53 | Sub-Subject: 53-00-general**

---

## Overview

This section covers the general aspects of fuselage structure for the AMPEL360 Q100 aircraft. It establishes the framework for fuselage design, construction, maintenance, and repair.

---

## Scope

| Area | Coverage |
|------|----------|
| Structure | Primary and secondary structure |
| Skin | Fuselage skin and doublers |
| Frames | Frames, stringers, longerons |
| Splices | Structural joints and splices |
| Maintenance | Inspection and repair procedures |
