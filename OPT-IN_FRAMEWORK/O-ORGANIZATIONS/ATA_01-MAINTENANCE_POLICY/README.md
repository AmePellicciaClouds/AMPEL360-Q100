# ATA 01 - MAINTENANCE POLICY

**Maintenance Policies and Procedures**

## Description

ATA Chapter 01 establishes the maintenance policy framework for the AMPEL360 Q100 aircraft. This chapter defines the maintenance philosophy, intervals, and organizational requirements for maintaining continued airworthiness.

## Scope

This chapter includes:

- Maintenance program philosophy and approach
- Scheduled maintenance requirements
- Maintenance planning and scheduling policies
- Maintenance organization and responsibilities
- Quality assurance requirements
- Documentation and record-keeping policies
- Maintenance manual organization
- Regulatory compliance framework
